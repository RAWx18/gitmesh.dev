🛠 Tools

Below you'll find all our core tools. Check out this internal sheet for a comprehensive list of all active subscriptions.

General

Communication

  • Slack: for internal communication.
  • Discord: the heart of our community.
  • Gmail: for email if necessary.
  • Loom: for videos (YouTube, Customer Support, internal explanations).

Documentation

  • GitBook: for long-term documentation.
  • Google Docs: notes, project documentation, formal and private documents.
  • ReadMe: Product & Developer Docs (will be migrated to GitBook).
  • Notion: legacy documentation, short-term notes, and private documents (currently migrating to Google Docs).

Compliance & Security

  • 1Password: for password management.
  • Drata: for policies and security training.
  • OnSecurity: Vulnerability and Penetration tests.

Analytics

  • June: for product analytics.
  • Plausible: for website analytics.
  • Hotjar: for session recordings.
  • Metabase: for company-wide dashboard.

HR

  • Remote: for employee time-off, payslips, expenses, documents, etc.
  • Checkr: for background checks
  • Join: for hiring.

Others

  • Linear: for project management and issue tracking.
  • Grammarly: for spelling and grammar checking.
  • Chatgitmesh: our distribution of ChatGPT.
  • Cabal: for monthly investor updates
  • Miro: for brainstorming (e.g., retrospectives, post-mortems)
  • Docsend: for document signing and distribution

Engineering & Product

Code, infrastructure, and observability

  • GitHub: where our code lives.
  • AWS: for infrastructure.
  • Postman: for API design and testing.
  • Logz: for logs and observability.
  • Admin Panel: for integration and customer plan monitoring.

Design

  • Figma: for product and marketing designs.
  • ScreenStudio: product videos incl. changelog content.

Billing

  • Stripe: billing and invoicing.

Go-To-Market

Sales & Marketing

  • HubSpot: as CRM and marketing automation platform.
  • Ahrefs: for SEO analysis and improvement.
  • Riverside: for podcast recording.
  • Webflow: for our website.

Customer Support

  • Plain: for customer support.

Owned Social Media & Content Distribution

  • Twitter
  • LinkedIn
  • YouTube
  • DEV.to
  • Medium
  • HackerNews
  • Reddit
  • ProductHunt
